Privacy Is About Autonomy.

I have dedicated my professional life to the pursuit of privacy as autonomy — We don't need to defend ourselves because we have "something to hide" but because we have someone to be.

Surveillance Is A Human Problem.

Tech trends have been amplified by the COVID-19 pandemic, as exemplified by camera, biometric, and location surveillance under the guise of public health solutions.

People are pushing back, however. As increased monitoring makes the link between privacy and autonomy crystal clear, entire populations are rejecting dystopian systems like facial recognition.
